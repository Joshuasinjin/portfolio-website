My Story

I grew up around a lot of artists and musicians. Naturally, I latched on to my surroundings and fell in love with the art scene. Music took the main hold. Over the years, although I loved writing and performing music, I found myself unable to cope with the music scene. Continuous live performances, almost always in bars, can bring a certain chaos into your life. I needed a change of pace, to do something I love while continuing to make music on the side. I spent years working in the service industry without any idea of what I wanted to do. I was introduced to UI design through my brother, a computer programmer. I did some research and it instantly peaked my interest. I fell in love with the process of designing user interfaces. I love designing something that most people don’t even think about; something that is imperative yet not necessarily acknowledged. There’s a subtle beauty to making art that effects people without them even knowing it.
