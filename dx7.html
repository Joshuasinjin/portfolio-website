DX7

DX7 came about from research and implementations of the 80’s. Custom icons and a vibrant colour palette is the backbone of the DX7 feeling.

My Process

Research

There’s not a lot of popular music streaming services out there. The two I focused on were Deezer and Spotify. My research helped me incorporate new ideas and ones I think Deezer and Spotify do well. One example would be a space to connect with friends and share music.

Ideation

Inspiration for DX7 came from the 80’s vibrancy and synth culture. The Yamaha DX7 is described as the sound of the 80’s as most major musicians at the time used one. This is what drove the design of DX7.

Wireframe

As this was one of my first projects, I found myself sketching a lot more than the others. Many different iterations eventual gave me a final piece to test and in turn incorporate into digital versions in Sketch.

Conclusion

DX7 will continue to change over time as I get more experienced in UI design. Overall I’m happy with this project and it taught me a lot about working efficiently. This project took me a very long time and in turn forced me to learn how to use Sketch in a more efficient way.
