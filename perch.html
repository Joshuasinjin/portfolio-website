Perch

Perch was created mainly from market and art research. Using vector points to come up with a custom logo was a big part of the process as well.

My Process

Research

Maps apps are everywhere. I found myself having more than enough apps to research and I tried to hone in on a couple. Roadtripper and Google Maps, helped me incorporate a maps design that works well and is familiar to users.

Ideation

The main inspiration for this project came about from wanting an app where I could find information on parks and beaches, without having to sift through reviews to find the information I was looking for.

Wireframe

Sketching was pretty quickly incorporated into its digital versions. I had a good reference from other maps apps which made testing and wireframing really easy and quick.
